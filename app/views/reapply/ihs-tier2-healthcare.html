Is the applicant eligibile for a Health and Care visa?

The applicant must work for, or have a job offer from, the NHS, an organisation providing medical services to the NHS, or an organisation providing adult social care.

The applicant must be a qualified:
  • doctor
  • nurse
  • health professional
  • adult social car profesional
