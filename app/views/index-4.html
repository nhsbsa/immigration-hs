Apply for your immigration health surcharge reimbursement

{{releaseVersion | log }}

The online IHS application is used by certain types of visa holders to apply for a refund of the IHS payment they paid as part of their visa application. Certain roles within health and social care and student status make user eligible to apply using this online service.

Within the journey, users need to upload proof that they hold one of these roles, as well as supporting documentation. This commonly takes place as job contract and payslips for health and social care, and GHIC and proof of student status for students.
