You cannot add your dependant to this claim

The dependant's claim period must be the same as the main applicant's.

If your dependant has a claim start date which is later than your own, you must miss them out from this claim. You can add them to your next claim.

Example

Main applicant claim period: 1 January 2022 to 30 June 2022

Dependant claim period: 1 March 2022 to 31 August 2022

You must miss your dependant from the January to June claim, and begin adding them on from the main applicant's July claim.

This is because we do not accept claims for less than 6 months, or claims which are overlapping with another claim period.

You can continue with your claim without adding a dependant.
