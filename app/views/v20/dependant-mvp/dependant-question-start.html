What are you applying for?

You can also add any dependants to your application. Their refund will be processed after your claim has been approved and paid, which can take up to 10 weeks.
Choose this option if you have already had a claim approved and paid as a main applicant, and you want to add a dependant to the same application.
Who is the main applicant?
The main applicant is the person working in the health and social care sector, which means they are eligible for an IHS reimbursement. This eligibility means their dependants can also apply for a reimbursement.
