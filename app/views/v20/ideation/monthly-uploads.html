Important

Upload evidence for the period: 30 April 2020 to 29 October 2020.

If the applicant does not have the correct evidence, their claim for reimbursement may be rejected or declined.

From each of the applicant's health and social care employers, we'll need a range of payslips for every day, in the 6 month period that the claim is being made

To help us with the claim, the applicant should make sure to upload payslips before and after the date period they wish to make a claim for.

We'll need a scanned copy or photo of the original documents, these must include the applicant's:

  • employee name
  • employer name
  • National Insurance number
  • pay date or period covered
  • employment start date
  • contracted hours

Uploaded evidence must be:

  • clear and readable
  • descriptive, with the file name containing the period that the evidence covers such as 'payslip-090123-150123.pdf'
  • JPG, JPEG, BMP, PNG or PDF
  • smaller than 2MB in size for single files
