Important

The contract you upload must include an employment start date which is before or on the start date of the claim period you are applying for: 10 April 2020

From each of the applicant's health and social care employers, we'll need a contract of employment, which shows an average of 16 hours or more per week.

! Warning Conditional contract documents or offer letters will not be accepted and will delay or reject your application

We'll need a scanned copy or photo of the original documents, these must include the applicant's:

  • employee name
  • employer name
  • National Insurance number
  • pay date or period covered
  • employment start date
  • contracted hours

Uploaded evidence must be:

  • clear and readable
  • descriptive, with the file name such as 'health-role-contract.pdf'
  • JPG, JPEG, BMP, PNG or PDF
  • smaller than 2MB in size for single files
