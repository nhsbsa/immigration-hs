You have not uploaded payslips covering every day of this claim. You need to upload alternative evidence to explain why there are gaps in the payslips.

If the applicant does not have payslips for some periods when they were working

For example, because they were paid in arrears for the first month in their role.

If the applicant does not have payslips for all the time they worked, you need to upload a signed letter from their employer that confirms the applicant's:

  • employment start date
  • contracted hours

If the applicant has gaps in their employment

They could have gaps in their employment because of:

  • maternity or paternity leave
  • COVID-19, for example, being furloughed
  • sickness, including self-isolation

If the applicant has gaps in their employment, you need to upload a letter from the applicant's employer confirming the exact dates of those gaps.
