Eligibility check Is the applicant carrying out any paid work in the UK?

This would include the dependant having worked, working or having an intention to work in the UK for the duration of their visa or European Health Insurance Card (EHIC) from 1 January 2021.

! Warning Working in the UK will invalidate your EHIC and you may have to pay for any NHS healthcare you receive.
What classes as paid work

Paid work includes:

  • any employment or self-employment which is liable for National Insurance, even if the minimum earnings threshold for owing National Insurance is not met
  • PHD students or other students, teaching as part of their course, will be classed as carrying out paid work due to having a contract of employment with their university
  • students undertaking a paid placement year in industry or any subsequent years of study

Paid work does not include:

  • unpaid work, unpaid work placements and volunteering
  • a scholarship income, such as a bursary, grant, travel expenses or stipend, as it is not considered taxable income
