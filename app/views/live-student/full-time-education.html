Eligibility check Is the applicant a full-time student in higher education?

Higher education takes place at universities and further education colleges. They normally include undergraduate and postgraduate study.

What classes as full-time education

Full-time education is where an average of more than 12 hours per week is spent during term time:

  • receiving tuition
  • engaging in practical work
  • receiving supervised study
  • taking examinations
This does not include apprenticeships.
