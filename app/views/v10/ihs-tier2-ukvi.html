The applicant is not eligible for an immigration health surcharge reimbursement

The applicant may be entitled to a Tier 2 Health and Care visa refund. If the applicant thinks they're eligible, email the refunds team IHSrefunds@homeoffice.gov.uk

In the email, include the applicant's:

  • name
  • sponsor's name
  • Certificate of Sponsorship (CoS) number
  • Immigration health surcharge number

Do not send your bank details. All refunds will be made using the payment method the applicant paid their immigration health surcharge with.


What happens next

We'll email you within 6 weeks to let you know the outcome.
