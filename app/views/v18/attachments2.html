The applicant must provide payslips as evidence, from all of the applicant's health and social care employers, for each month of the 6-month period the claim is being made for. We need these payslip so we can match the applicant with the eligibility criteria.

Things to remember when uploading payslips
  • your payslip attachments must be: jpg, jpeg, bmp, png or pdf and no larger than 2MB in size
  • you upload the correct amount of payslips to cover the claim period
  • you upload payslips that correspond to the claim period entered
  • the payslips are not password-protected
