Register for an IHS reimbursement account

Once you've registered for an IHS reimbursement account, you'll be able to:

  • apply for an IHS reimbursement
  • update your personal details
  • update your employment or student information
  • check application and payment statuses

Registering takes around 5 minutes.

Before you start

We'll ask you for your:

  • email address
  • mobile number
  • date of birth
  • IHS number
  • national insurance number

We'll also ask for your National Insurance number, if you have one.
