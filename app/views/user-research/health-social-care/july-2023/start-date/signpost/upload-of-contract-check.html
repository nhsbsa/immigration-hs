Application Does the applicant have employment evidence for the period: 30 April 2022 to 29 October 2022?

From each of the applicant's health and social care employers, we'll need both a:

  • range of payslips for every day, in the 6 month period that the claim is being made
  • contract of employment, which shows an average of 16 hours or more per week
To help us with the claim, the applicant should make sure to upload payslips before and after the date period they wish to make a claim for.

We'll need a scanned copy or photo of the original documents, these must include the applicant's:

  • employee name
  • employer name
  • National Insurance number
  • pay date or period covered
  • employment start date
  • contracted hours
