Eligibility check Is the applicant carrying out any paid work in the UK?

This would include the applicant working or having an intention to work in the UK for the duration of their visa or European Health Insurance Card (EHIC) from 1 January 2021.

What classes as paid work

Paid work is any employment or self-employment which is liable for National Insurance, even if the minimum earnings threshold for owing National Insurance is not met.

Unpaid work, unpaid work placements and volunteering are allowed. 'Scholarship income', such as a bursary, is not considered taxable income so someone in receipt of this would remain eligible for the reimbursement.

For PHD and other students who may teach as part of their course, this will constitute paid work where they hold a contract of employment with their university.
