The dependant is not eligible for an immigration health surcharge reimbursement

Based on your answers, the dependant is not eligible to use this service right now as they do not meet the eligibility criteria.

The dependant can use this service if they:

    • have paid an immigration health surcharge for their visa
    • have a visa begining on or after the 1st of January 2021
    • have a valid European Health Insurace Card (EHIC) from an EU country
    • are not undertaking paid work in the UK or intending to undertake paid work in the UK
    • are currently living in the UK
