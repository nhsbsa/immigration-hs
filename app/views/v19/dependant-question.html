Application Does the applicant want to add any dependants who have a valid European Health Insurance Card (EHIC) issued from the EU, Switzerland, Norway, Iceland or Liechtenstein?

The applicant's dependants can be their:

  • spouse or civil partner
  • children
  • durable partner (an unmarried partnership that has normally continued for 2 years or more)
What dependant information will be needed

The required information includes the dependant's:

  • full name
  • date of birth
  • immigration health surcharge (IHS) number
  • visa share code
  • UK address
  • scan, photo or screenshot of their European Health Insurance Card (EHIC)
