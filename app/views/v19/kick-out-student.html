The applicant is not eligible for an immigration health surcharge reimbursement

Based on your answers, the applicant is not eligible to use this service right now as they do not meet the eligibility criteria.

The applicant can use this service if they:

  • have paid an immigration health surcharge for their visa
  • have a visa beginning on or after the 1st of January 2021
  • have a valid European Health Insurance Card (EHIC) issued from an EEA Country or Switzerland
  • are a full-time student in higher education
  • are not undertaking paid work in the UK or intending to undertake paid work in the UK
  • are currently living in the UK
