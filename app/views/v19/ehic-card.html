Eligibility check Does the applicant have a valid European Health Insurance Card (EHIC) issued from the EU, Switzerland, Norway, Iceland or Liechtenstein ?

A European Health Insurance Card (EHIC) entitles the holder to access medically necessary, state-provided healthcare.

The EHIC must be valid for the whole period of the applicant's claim. If it's not valid for the whole period, the applicant may get a partial reimbursement.

Find out more information about EHIC on the European Commission website (opens in new tab).
