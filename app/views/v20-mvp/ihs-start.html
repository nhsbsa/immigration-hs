Important

We've made changes to how you upload evidence for your application.

Before you start

You will need:

  • your National Insurance number
  • your email address
  • the name of the organisation you work for
  • your IHS number for the period you are applying for and IHS numbers for any dependants you apply for
  • copies of your payslips for your full claim period
  • a copy of your job contract

Evidence you need

You will need to submit evidence as part of your application that confirms you have worked in an eligible role for at least 6 months and for an average of at least 16 hours a week.

We will ask you for:

  • copies of your payslips covering every day in the period you are claiming for
  • a copy of your job contract

Your payslips

You will need to upload copies of your payslips that cover every day in the period you are claiming for.

Example

If you are claiming a refund for the period from 6 April 2021 to 5 October 2021, you will need to upload payslips for April, May, June, July, August, September and the first 5 days in October.

Missing payslips

If you do not have payslips covering every day in your claim period, you will need to upload a signed letter from your employer that explains why there are gaps in your payslips. For example, because you were paid in arrears for the first month in your job.

Your job contract

You will need to upload a copy of your job contract for your eligible role. If you have more than one job in health or social care, you can upload multiple contracts.

Gaps in employment

If you have any gaps in your employment during the claim period, for example, because of maternity leave or sick leave, you will also need to submit evidence covering those gaps. This could be a letter from your employer confirming the exact dates of your leave.
