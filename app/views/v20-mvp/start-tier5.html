If you have a Tier 5 Medical Training Initiative (MTI) visa

You may be able to get money back if you paid the immigration health surcharge (IHS) and you are a member of the Medical Training Initiative (MTI)

Tier 5 MTI visa holders can apply for a refund of their immigration health surcharge (IHS)

Once the applicant used this service, the academy then checks that the applicant has an MTI visa and certificate of sponsorship.

Applicant details will then be passed to us and they (and any listed dependants) will get a full refund of their IHS, instead of getting 6 months' worth of their refund that they would get by using the Health and Social Care route.

The NHS Business Services Authority will contact the applicant with an outcome. The applicant does not need to reapply.
