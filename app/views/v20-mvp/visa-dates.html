Application What are the dates of the visa associated with this IHS claim?

These are the start and end dates of the visa you held during the period you're claiming for.

Where can I find my visa dates?
For a current visa, dates can be found on your eVisa document. This is available online through your UKVI account.

For expired visas, dates can be found on old visa documentation. See our Knowledge Base for more information about where to find visa dates.
Where can I find my visa dates?
For a current visa, dates can be found on your eVisa document. This is available online through your UKVI account.

For expired visas, dates can be found on:
  • the Home Office decision letter (grant letter)
  • the visa vignette (sticker) in the passport, if one was issued for that visa
  • the previous Biometric Residence Permit (BRP), if the visa was issued before the move to eVisas
  • an official Home Office email or PDF confirming the visa grant
! Warning If your IHS claim dates are outside the visa dates you provide, your claim will be rejected.
