Refunds for health and care staff

If you work in health and care and would now be eligible for a Tier 2 (General) Health and Care visa

Customers who paid the IHS on or after 31 March

You and your dependants do not need to pay the IHS if you are applying for a Tier 2 (General) Health and Care visa. If you paid the IHS on or after 31 March 2020, and would have been eligible for the Tier 2 (General) Health and Care visa from 4 August, you will receive a full refund.

Customers who paid the IHS before 31 March 2020

If you have a Tier 2 visa and are in a role that would now fall under the Tier 2 (General) Health and Care visa – in general a healthcare professional role, such as doctor, nurse, allied health professional or social worker- and you paid the IHS before 31 March 2020, you may be eligible for a refund of your IHS and any paid for your dependents.

Refunds will be made in relation to any 6-month blocks of IHS paid that are remaining after 31 March, for example, if you have paid your IHS until 12 November 2020, you will receive a 6 month refund of £200 (or currency equivalent).

If have not yet received a refund, email the IHS refunds team at IHSrefunds@homeoffice.gov.uk. In your mail, include your:

  • name
  • sponsor's name
  • Certificate of Sponsorship (CoS) number
  • IHS number
Do not send your bank details. All refunds will be paid to the account or card you paid with when you made your IHS payment.

If you are not eligible for a Tier 2 (General) Health and Care Visa

If you work in health and care, you can apply for a refund of any IHS paid to cover the period from 31 March 2020.

You must have worked in an eligible health or care role for at least 6 months since 31 March 2020, working for an average of at least 16 hours a week.

If you meet these conditions, your dependants are also entitled to a refund.

What you'll get

Each time you apply, you will get back 6 months of the IHS you have paid. You will need to apply every 6 months.

You can only get back the money for the IHS that covers the period since 31 March 2020.

Apply for an IHS reimbursement if you work in health and care.

You will normally receive your money back within 6 weeks of a successful application.

Please check your bank account before contacting us about a payment that has not been received.
