Who can get a refund

You may be able to get money back if you paid the immigration health surcharge (IHS) and you work in a health or care setting or organisation.

How you apply to get money back depends on your visa and what type of job you have.

Medical professionals with a Tier 2 (General) visa

You are eligible to apply for a refund if you both:

  • have a Tier 2 (General) visa
  • work as a medical professional

Find out how to apply as a medical professional with a Tier 2 visa.

Medical professionals without a Tier 2 visa

If you are a medical professional but you do not have a Tier 2 visa, you need to apply for a refund the same way as other roles in the health and care sector.

Other roles in health and care settings

You may be eligible for a refund if you work in a health or social care setting. Your role does not have to be in frontline care. For example, receptionist in a GP office or a digital role.

Find out how to apply for a refund if you work in a health or care setting.
