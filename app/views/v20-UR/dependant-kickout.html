You need to make a separate claim for your dependant

If your dependant has a different claim period start date then you need to make apply for them separately after making a claim for the main applicant.

You can continue with this claim for 1 January 2022 to 30 June 2022, but leave out your dependant and use our dependant journey to make a separate dependant application using their claim period dates.

You should wait until you receive a claim approval email from us before claiming for a dependant.

Example

Main applicant claim period: 1 January 2022 to 30 June 2022

Dependant claim period: 1 March 2022 to 31 August 2022

You must miss your dependant from this January to June claim, and make a separate claim for March to August.

You will need to provide the evidence needed for the full claim period.

You can continue with your claim without adding a dependant.
