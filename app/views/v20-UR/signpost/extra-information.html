Application Add extra information or upload evidence about gaps in the applicant's employment or working arrangements

! Warning Do not include personal or financial information, like a National Insurance number or credit card details.
Give as much information as possible, including start and end dates of the gaps.
You can enter up to 2000 characters

If possible, the applicant should upload evidence to support their extra information.

This can be a letter from their employer, such as:

  • an employer's agreement
  • or a letter about any maternity, paternity, family care, or illness


Uploaded evidence must be:

  • clear and readable
  • descriptive, with the file name containing the period that the evidence covers such as 'maternity-letter-090123-150123.pdf'
  • JPG, JPEG, BMP, PNG or PDF
  • no larger than 2MB in size for single files
