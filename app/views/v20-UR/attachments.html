The applicant must provide payslips as evidence, from all of the applicant's health and social care employers, for each month of the 6-month period the claim is being made for. We need these payslips so we can match the applicant with the eligibility criteria.

Evidence can be uploaded as scanned copies, digital photographs or digital payslips.

Attach evidence for the period between 31 March 2020 to 30 September 2020.  
Things to remember when uploading evidence
  • your uploaded attachments must be: jpg, jpeg, bmp, png or pdf and no larger than 2MB in size
  • you upload the correct amount of payslips to cover the claim period
  • you upload payslips that correspond to the claim period entered
  • the payslips are not password-protected
