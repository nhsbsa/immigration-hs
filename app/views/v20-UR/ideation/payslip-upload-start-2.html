Claim period

Payslips must cover every day in the period from 20 April 2020 to 119 October 2020.

You can upload as many documents as you need to cover this time including payslips from before and after the claim period.

If the applicant has more than one job in health or care, we'll need payslips for all their health or care jobs.

Payslips must include the applicant's:

  • name
  • employer's name
  • National insurance number
  • pay date or period covered by the payslip
  • employment start date
  • contracted hours

Files

You must send scanned copies or photos of the applicant's original documents.

Files must:

  • have names that include the time they cover, for example, 'August 2020 payslip'
  • be clear and readable
  • be in JPG, JPEG, BMP, PNG or PDF format
  • be smaller than 2MB each
