The applicant is not eligible for an immigration health surcharge reimbursement but may be entitled to a refund

The applicant may be entitled to a refund if they're a:

  • Tier 2 visa holder
  • health professional

If the applicant thinks they're eligible, they can find out more by visiting the Tier 2 visa medical professional section of the immigration health surcharge refund webpage on GOV.UK.
