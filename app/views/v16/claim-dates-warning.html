Application What's the starting date of the 6-month period the applicant is claiming for?

The applicant can only claim a reimbursement for a 6 month period.
Due to legal changes, this date must be on or after 31 March 2020. Once a claim start date is entered, we'll automatically work out the end date of the 6-month period the applicant is claiming for.
For example, 15 8 2020
! Warning The previous claim was for the period 31 May 2020 to 30 November 2020

There'll be an option to use a reminder service that will notify the applicant that they may need to reapply.
