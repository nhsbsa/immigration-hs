The applicant is a Tier 5 Medical Training Initiative visa holder

The applicant is a Tier 5 MTI visa holder and does not need to apply for a refund of their IHS. Refunds will be requested on behalf of MTI visa holders by the Academy of Medical Royal Colleges (AoMRC) and processed by UK Visas and Immigration (UKVI). Notification will be sent to the visa holder when payment of the refund has been made. The applicant does not need to do anything else.
