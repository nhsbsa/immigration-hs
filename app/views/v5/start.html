Get a reimbursement for your immigration health surcharge

Who can apply

To be eligible for using this service, the applicant must be working or have previously worked in a health and social care sector job:

  • for 6 months or more
  • for an average of 16 hours per week
  • on or after 31 March 2020
A person who is eligible to use this service will be the applicant. The applicant’s dependants will also be automatically eligible.

How to apply

To use this service, the applicant must apply. If you are using the service on behalf of an applicant, use their details.

Claims made will be for a period of 6 months. The applicant will need to reapply after every 6 months to continue getting reimbursed.

You’ll need:

  • to give us your full name as it appears on your passport
  • your immigration health surcharge number
  • your National Insurance number
  • payslips for the last 6 months, these can be attached as copies
