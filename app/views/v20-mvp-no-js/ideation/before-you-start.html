Before you start

You will need:

  • Your personal information such as name and date of birth
  • Your national insurance number
  • Your IHS number
  • An email address
  • Your dependant information if you are applying for them
  • The claim starting date
  • Your employment information such as employer, job title and job setting
  • Your payslips covering the period you are claiming for
  • Your contract for the roles you have told us you worked in over the claim period
  • Extra evidence if you have any gaps in employment over the claim period
